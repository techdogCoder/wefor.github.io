Who we are

Founded in 2017, WeFor is a leading digital partner to global brand owners. Based in Shanghai, China, we bind marketing and technology and provide a full suite of enterprise digitalized transformation, clienteling solution, omni-channel data intelligence service.

With a proven track record serving top premium brands in the verticals of FMCG，luxury, fashion and beauty in the APAC and Greater China markets, our professional and international team endeavor to help brand owners to fulfill truly global digital retail footprints.
